Garlic Fried Rice by Futuredish

Description

A simple but delicious fried rice filled with the flavour of garlic! You only need 5 ingredient max -- simply perfect for the typical college student. Try it out!

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Soy Sauce
  • Oil
  • Rice
  • Butter

Steps

  1. In a hot pan filled with oil, add 1 tbsp of garlic.
  2. After the garlic starts to turn brown, add 2 tbsp of soy sauce. Stir momentarily, then add 1 cup of rice.
  3. Once the rice is properly coated, bring them to one side of the pan. Place 1 tbsp of butter on the other side and let it melt momentarily.
  4. Stir fry for about 3 mins, letting the garlic and butter juices to work into the grains of the rice.
  5. Et voila! Enjoy your meal!
