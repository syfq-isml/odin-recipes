Egg Fried Rice by Futuredish

Description

Got 5 minutes? Satiate your hunger with this simple and quick egg fried rice recipe. Minimal ingredients for the minimalist chef.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Rice
  • Oil
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Spring Onion

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, mix 2 eggs with 1 cup of cooked rice. Add 1/2 tbsp of oil, stir momentarily then set aside.
  2. In a hot pan, add 1 tbsp of oil. Once the oil is hot, add in spring onions. Stir fry until fragrant.
  3. Add in the rice from Step 1. Stir until egg is well-cooked.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. You're done!
